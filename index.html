Hello there, I am a research software engineer at IBM Watson, where I work with Dr. Bowen Zhou on artificial neural networks and natural language processing. My research interest is neural networks with external memory and/or attention. Currently I am leading a small team in Watson working on machine reading comprehension and reasoning. We focus on:

  • End-to-end recurrent neural networks augmented with Memory and Attention
  • algorithm learning
  • Multi-task learning
  • reinfocement learning
  • Neural machine comprehension and Natural language inference and reasoning
  • Structured prediction
